TechVision AI Platform - Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions:

Q: What is the TechVision AI Platform?
A: The TechVision AI Platform is a comprehensive suite of artificial intelligence tools and services designed to help businesses integrate AI capabilities into their applications. It includes natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, and automated machine learning features.

Q: Who can use the TechVision AI Platform?
A: Our platform is designed for businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprise organizations. Whether you're a developer, data scientist, or business analyst, our tools are built to be accessible and powerful.

Q: Do I need machine learning expertise to use your platform?
A: No! While machine learning knowledge is helpful, our platform includes AutoML features that automate much of the complex work. We also provide extensive documentation, tutorials, and support to help you get started.

Technical Questions:

Q: What programming languages are supported?
A: Our platform provides SDKs for Python, JavaScript, Java, and Go. We also offer REST APIs that can be used with any programming language.

Q: How do I integrate the platform with my existing application?
A: Integration is simple using our APIs and SDKs. Most developers can complete a basic integration in under an hour. We provide quickstart guides for popular frameworks like React, Django, and Spring Boot.

Q: What is the API rate limit?
A: Rate limits depend on your plan:
- Starter: 100 requests per minute
- Professional: 1,000 requests per minute
- Enterprise: Custom limits based on your needs

Q: Can I deploy models on-premises?
A: Yes, enterprise customers can deploy models on-premises or in their own cloud environment. Contact our sales team for more information.

Q: What data formats are supported?
A: We support JSON, CSV, and various image formats (JPEG, PNG, TIFF) for computer vision tasks. Audio files in WAV and MP3 formats are supported for speech recognition.

Pricing and Billing:

Q: How does pricing work?
A: We offer tiered pricing based on usage:
- Starter Plan: $99/month for up to 10,000 API calls
- Professional Plan: $499/month for up to 100,000 API calls
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing for high-volume usage

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with access to all features. No credit card required to start.

Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
A: You'll receive email notifications at 75% and 90% of your limit. Once exceeded, API calls will be throttled unless you upgrade your plan. No overage charges without your approval.

Q: Can I change my plan at any time?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades and at the next billing cycle for downgrades.

Security and Compliance:

Q: How is my data secured?
A: We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. All data is stored in SOC 2 Type II certified data centers.

Q: Is the platform GDPR compliant?
A: Yes, we are fully GDPR compliant. We provide data processing agreements and tools for managing user data according to GDPR requirements.

Q: Do you use my data to train your models?
A: No, we never use customer data to train our models unless explicitly agreed upon in writing. Your data remains your data.

Q: Where is data stored?
A: We have data centers in the US, EU, and Asia. You can choose where your data is stored during account setup.

Performance and Reliability:

Q: What is your uptime guarantee?
A: We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all paid plans, with full refunds for any downtime beyond this threshold.

Q: How fast are API responses?
A: Average response times are under 200ms for most API calls. Complex operations like model training may take longer.

Q: Can the platform handle traffic spikes?
A: Yes, our infrastructure auto-scales to handle traffic spikes. Enterprise customers can also pre-warm capacity for planned events.

Support Questions:

Q: What support options are available?
A: All customers have access to:
- Email support with 24-hour response time
- Documentation and tutorials
- Community forum

Professional and Enterprise customers also receive:
- Priority email support (2-hour response time)
- Phone support
- Dedicated account manager (Enterprise only)

Q: Do you offer training for my team?
A: Yes! We offer online training courses and can arrange custom training sessions for Enterprise customers.

Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?
A: You can report bugs or request features through our support portal, email, or community forum. We actively review all feedback.
